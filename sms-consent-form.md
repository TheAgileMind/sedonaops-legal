# SedonaOps Contractor SMS Consent

**Program:** SedonaOps Cleaning Staff Notifications

---

## How Contractors Opt In to SMS Notifications

SedonaOps cleaning staff consent to receive SMS notifications through **two verified methods**, depending on whether they are new or existing contractors.

---

## Method 1: Contractor Services Agreement (New Staff Onboarding)

All new cleaning contractors complete the SedonaOps Contractor Services Agreement before beginning work. The agreement includes a **Communication Consent** section that must be initialed separately:

### Communication Consent Section

> **SMS Communication Consent**
>
> I, \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_, consent to receive SMS messages from SedonaOps regarding:
>
> - Cleaning schedules and appointment times
> - Job confirmations and reminders
> - Payment notifications
> - Property maintenance issue reports
> - Operational updates related to my assigned properties
>
> **I understand that:**
>
> - Message frequency: 2-10 messages per day during active booking periods
> - Message and data rates may apply per my wireless carrier
> - I can reply **STOP** at any time to opt out of SMS notifications
> - I can reply **HELP** for assistance
> - My information is handled per the [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy)
> - Full service terms are available at [Terms and Conditions](terms-and-conditions)
>
> **Phone Number:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_
>
> **Initials:** \_\_\_\_\_\_ **Date:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

The contractor must:
1. Provide their phone number
2. Initial the SMS consent section
3. Sign the full Contractor Services Agreement

**No messages are sent until the agreement is signed.**

---

## Method 2: SMS Keyword Opt-In (Existing Staff Verification)

Existing contractors who were onboarded before the SMS notification system can opt in via text message:

**Step 1:** Contractor texts **START** or **EMPEZAR** to the SedonaOps number.

**Step 2:** SedonaOps replies with the full disclosure:

> SedonaOps: You're now registered for cleaning schedule notifications. Msg frequency: 2-10/day during active bookings. Msg&Data rates may apply. Reply HELP for assistance or STOP to unsubscribe. Privacy: https://theagilemind.github.io/sedonaops-legal/privacy-policy Terms: https://theagilemind.github.io/sedonaops-legal/terms-and-conditions

**Step 3:** Contractor is enrolled and receives their first notification.

---

## Required Disclosures (Present in Both Methods)

| Disclosure | Present | Details |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Business Name | Yes | SedonaOps / Project Sedona PMS |
| Program Description | Yes | Cleaning schedules, payments, issue reports |
| Message Frequency | Yes | 2-10 messages per day during active bookings |
| Cost Disclosure | Yes | "Message and data rates may apply" |
| Opt-Out Instructions | Yes | Reply STOP to unsubscribe |
| Help Instructions | Yes | Reply HELP for assistance |
| Privacy Policy | Yes | [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy) |
| Terms and Conditions | Yes | [Terms and Conditions](terms-and-conditions) |

---

## Opt-In Methods NOT Used

SedonaOps does **not** collect consent through:

- Verbal consent without written record
- Paper-only forms without digital backup
- Imported or purchased contact lists
- Website chat widgets or pop-ups
- Social media opt-ins
- Pre-checked consent boxes

---

## Bilingual Support

SedonaOps serves a bilingual cleaning team (English and Spanish). Opt-in keywords are supported in both languages:

- **English:** START / STOP / HELP
- **Spanish:** EMPEZAR / STOP / HELP
