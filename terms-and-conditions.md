# SedonaOps SMS Terms and Conditions

**Last Updated: February 4, 2026**

## Program Information

**Program Name:** SedonaOps Cleaning Staff Notifications

**Program Description:** SedonaOps provides SMS notifications to contracted vacation rental cleaning staff. Messages include cleaning schedules, job reminders, arrival/completion confirmations, payment notifications, and property issue communications. This is a two-way conversational service where staff can respond to messages.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your assigned cleaning jobs and schedule. You can expect to receive:
- **Typical frequency:** 2-10 messages per week during active periods
- **Reminders:** 1-2 messages per scheduled cleaning
- **Confirmations:** 1 message per completed job
- **Payment notifications:** 1-2 messages per payment cycle

Message frequency may increase during busy seasons or decrease during slower periods.

## Message and Data Rates

**Message and data rates may apply.** Standard messaging rates from your wireless carrier will apply to messages sent and received. Contact your carrier for details about your messaging plan.

## Consent and Opt-In

By providing your phone number and signing the SedonaOps Contractor Services Agreement, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your cleaning assignments. You confirm that:
- You are the owner or authorized user of the mobile phone number provided
- You agree to receive work-related SMS notifications from SedonaOps
- You understand this is a two-way conversational service

## How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time.

**To opt out, reply STOP to any message.**

After opting out, you will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS notifications will be sent. Note that opting out may affect your ability to receive important job notifications.

To resume messages after opting out, reply **START** or contact us directly.

## How to Get Help

**For assistance, reply HELP to any message.**

You will receive a message with support contact information.

You can also contact us directly:
- **Email:** support@sedonaops.ai
- **Phone:** [Your business phone]
