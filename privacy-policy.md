# SedonaOps Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 4, 2026**

## Overview

SedonaOps ("we," "our," or "us") operates a property management platform that includes SMS notification services for contracted cleaning staff. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Phone Numbers
We collect phone numbers from contracted cleaning staff solely for the purpose of sending work-related SMS notifications.

### Message Data
We may store message history to:
- Confirm delivery of notifications
- Maintain records of job communications
- Resolve disputes or questions about assignments

### Job-Related Information
Messages may include:
- Cleaning schedules and reminders
- Property addresses and access information
- Payment confirmations
- Issue reports and follow-ups

## How We Use Your Information

Your phone number and related data are used **exclusively** for:
- Sending cleaning schedule notifications
- Confirming job completions
- Processing payment notifications
- Communicating about property issues
- Responding to your replies and questions

## Information Sharing

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your phone number or personal information to third parties
- Share your information for marketing purposes
- Use your data for any purpose unrelated to SedonaOps cleaning operations

**We may share information with:**
- Property owners (only job-related communications)
- Service providers necessary to deliver SMS (e.g., Twilio)
- Law enforcement if required by law

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:
- Encrypted data transmission
- Secure database storage
- Access controls limiting who can view your information

## Data Retention

We retain your phone number and message history for:
- The duration of your contractor relationship
- Up to 12 months after the relationship ends (for record-keeping)
- Longer if required by law or for dispute resolution

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Opt out** of SMS notifications at any time by replying **STOP**
- **Request information** about data we hold about you
- **Request deletion** of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Update** your phone number or contact preferences

## SMS Program Details

**Program Name:** SedonaOps Cleaning Staff Notifications

**Message Frequency:** Varies based on your assigned cleaning jobs. Typically 2-10 messages per week during active periods.

**Message & Data Rates:** Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

**Opt-Out:** Reply **STOP** to any message to unsubscribe from SMS notifications.

**Help:** Reply **HELP** for assistance or contact us at the information below.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via SMS or other means.
